Ash Wednesday

Submitted by: Matthew Conway

Ingredients

  • 2 ounces gin
  • 3 blackberries
  • 3/4 ounce heavy cream
  • 3/4 ounce honey syrup

Technique

  1. In a shaker, muddle the blackberries
  2. Add the gin, cream and honey syrup
  3. Fill with ice
  4. Shake vigorously
  5. Strain into a chilled coupe

Notes

  • In the absence of heavy cream, substitute half-and-half.
  • To avoid gunking up your shaker, combine two parts honey to one part hot water, stir to dissolve, cool, and refrigerate until needed.
  • Courtesy of Michael McIlroy; Milk & Honey, New York
