Poet's Dream

Submitted by: Matthew Conway

There are more than a few variations on this cocktail, but this one will surely endow its drinker with eloquence.

Ingredients

  • 2 ounces gin (bold, prominent juniper)
  • 3/4 ounce dry vermouth
  • 1/4 ounce Benedictine
  • 2 dashes orange bitters
  • 1 lemon twist

Technique

  1. Combine all ingredients in a mixing glass
  2. Fill with ice
  3. Stir until cold
  4. Strain into a chilled coupe or martini glass
  5. Garnish with the twist
  6. Wax poetic

Notes

  • Adapted by Meaghan Dorman; Lantern's Keep, New York
