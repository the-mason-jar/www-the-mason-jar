The Austin

Submitted by: D. Keith Robinson

Based off a version of the Pegu Club Cocktail, The Austin is the best type of gin drink; slightly sweet and slightly crisp with a nice, warm tingle on the end.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 Fresno chili
  • 1 1/2 oz gin
  • 1/2 oz Grand Marnier
  • 3/4 oz lime juice
  • 1/4 oz simple syrup
  • 2 dashes Angostura bitters

Technique

  1. Slice the chili and remove the seeds
  2. Muddle the chili and pour in the rest of the ingredients
  3. Add ice and shake vigorously
  4. Pour into a martini glass

Notes

  • You can make it without the chili and it's still really nice.
  • Will need to try this one with a hotter chili.
