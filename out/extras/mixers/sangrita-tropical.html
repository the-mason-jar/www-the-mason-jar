Sangrita Tropical

Submitted by: D. Keith Robinson

A bit more fruity tham the Sangrita Tradicional, this tropical delight is the perfect back to a nice tequila blanco.

Ingredients

  • 3 cup pineapple juice
  • 1/2 cup orange juice
  • 2 limes
  • 6 tbsp hot sauce

Preparation

  • Juice the lime.
  • Blend or mix until well combined.

Notes
